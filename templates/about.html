ABOUT

Creating Queue:

Creating a queue will give user access to the Admin page. The Admin page gives queue owner ability to see everyone in line and to move on to next person in queue. Creating a queue is as simple as pressing the big Create Queue button. The Admin page will pop up in another tab, while the main screen gives users the ability to Scab or Type the code to enter the queue.

Joining Queue:

Joining a Queue is as simple as pulling out yout smartphone and scaning the QR code with your camera. If your phone does no suppoer scanning QR codes or some other issue occures, you can join a queue manually by heading to Cyber Sequences Get in Queue page and entering in the credentials there.
